Education in Switzerland

  • School is compulsory from age 6 to 15
  • Famous universities include ETH Zurich and EPFL Lausanne (Both ranked amoung the top in the world)
  • Switzerland has one of the highest number of nobel prize winners per capita.
  • Education focuses on innovation, languages, and applied science
