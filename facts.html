Fun Facts About Switzerland

Slamming a Door Can Cost You 500$

In Switzerland, being too loud isnt just rude- it can be expensive! If you slam a door too hard and disturb your neibors, you coulf face a fine of up to 500%.Swiss people really value peace and quiet.

Escaping From Prison Isn't a Crime

Believe it or not, in Switzerland, escaping from prison isn't illegal by itself. The law sees the desire of freedom as a basic huan instinct- but of you damage property or hurt someone while escaping, you will still get punished.

You Can't Own Just One Guinea pig

It's illigal to own only one guinea pig in Switzerland- they get lonely , and animal welfare laws require them to live in pairs

Cheese Heaven

There are more than 450 types of Swiss cheese- from Emmental to Gruyere- enough to try one everyday for over one year!

60% of Country is Mountains

Most of Switzerland is covered by Alps, giving it some of the most beautiful and photographed landscapes on earth.

Switzerland is a wonderful country
